Don’t Board Me

Selection & Initial Impression

The website “Don’t Board Me” was awarded Site of the Day by Awwwards on March 11, 2024, a recognition that highlights its excellent design, user experience, and creativity. The site belongs to a home pet care company and stands out for its warm aesthetic and friendly communication, prioritizing emotional experience over complex technical effects. From the very first moment, the site conveys a sense of trust and tenderness. The composition is simple yet effective: a happy dog image, a clear message, and a clean, charming design. It doesn’t aim to impress with excessive animations but rather to create an emotional connection with the visitor through friendly doodles and illustrations. In addition, the warm color palette reinforces the idea of closeness and well-being.

Structure & Information Architecture

The information architecture is direct and logical. The menu includes sections like “Services,” “Pricing,” “About,” “Blog,” and “Contact,” plus a very clear “Book Now” button that stays visible. As you scroll, the narrative is smooth and engaging: first it explains what the company does in “Services,” and it also uses option carousels so you can switch between services. Then comes the step-by-step process “How it works”, which guides you on how to proceed. Finally, you see the testimonials and pricing.

Visual Design

The color palette is soft, featuring shades of red and pink, complemented by neutral and natural tones that enhance the pet images without being distracting. The typography is modern, legible, and consistent throughout the site. The design reinforces the identity of a friendly, professional, and empathetic brand. There are no overwhelming elements, everything feels airy and harmonious. Additionally, the illustrations are very cute and visually engaging.

Interaction & Microinteractions

The site doesn’t use 3D effects or WebGL animations, but it does include microinteractions and subtle animations: entry transitions, scroll movements, hover effects on buttons, and small details like the dog’s fur moving slightly. These gentle animations make the experience more enjoyable without distracting from the content.

Usability & Accessibility

The website is clear and intuitive. Everything is easily accessible: information, pricing, booking, and contact. The design prioritizes readability, contrast, and clean navigation. It’s also very well thought out for the average user, without overwhelming them with unnecessary or hard-to-use elements. On mobile devices, the design responds correctly, although the size of some images could slightly affect loading speed.

Technical Performance

As an award-winning site, “Don’t Board Me” meets high standards of performance and accessibility. However, since it contains many images, loading times can increase if they aren’t fully optimized. Navigation is smooth, and resources are well managed. According to the Lighthouse report (featured on Awwwards), the site scores highly in design, creativity, and UX.

Excellence Criteria

The design is outstanding, achieving a perfect balance between clean aesthetics and emotional purpose. Usability is excellent, intuitive and seamless. Creativity is moderate but effective, focusing more on empathy than on technical innovation. The content is substantial and well-structured, with concise, user-friendly text.

Conclusion

This site really shows how powerful simplicity can be when it’s combined with a strong emotional message. I especially loved the overall aesthetics, everything feels warm, balanced, and visually inviting. The subtle microinteractions add a nice touch of professionalism without making the experience heavy, and the clear hierarchy with visible calls to action makes it super easy to navigate. Still, it could improve a bit by optimizing images for faster loading on mobile, maybe tweaking contrasts.

Chipsa

Selection & Initial Impression

Chipsa design immediately establishes itself as a high-level creative studio, recognized internationally and awarded by platforms such as CSSDA, Behance, Awwwards, and FWA. Its tagline, “We evoke emotions through aesthetics, WebGL and 3D,” sets the expectation for an immersive, emotionally engaging experience. On first entering the site, the impact is striking: high-quality imagery, fluid animations, and sophisticated visuals create a sense of modernity and professionalism while inviting exploration. The design feels immersive rather than overwhelming, making users want to interact further.

Structure & Information Architecture

The site demonstrates a well-considered information architecture. The top menu includes “Cases,” “About Studio,” “Sites,” “Interfaces,” “Visuals,” and “Contacts,” providing immediate clarity about the studio’s offerings. The content is presented in a chaptered structure, with a “Choose Chapter” option on the bottom left, that allows users to navigate sections at their own pace. This approach organizes the experience logically: first showcasing work, then explaining philosophy and services, and finally presenting contact information and recognitions.

Visual Design

Chipsa design excels visually, balancing soberness and sophistication. A subtle color palette allows 3D imagery and CGI elements to remain the focus, while modern, clean typography ensures readability and complements the overall aesthetic. The use of 3D and WebGL is integral to the site’s identity, giving it a unique and recognizable style that communicates expertise in digital design rather than relying solely on conventional graphics.

Interaction & Microinteractions

The site’s interactivity enhances the experience without distracting from content. Animations and transitions are smooth and purposeful, reacting to cursor movement, scroll, and section changes. Microinteractions such as hover effects and subtle element shifts create a sense of depth and engagement, making the user feel as if they are navigating a tactile space rather than a static page. It really makes the user immerse into the webpage.

Usability & Accessibility

Despite the complexity of its visuals and 3D content, Chipsa design remains intuitive and user-friendly. Navigation is clear, the hierarchy is consistent, and users can locate information without confusion. On desktop, the experience is fluid, though heavy 3D assets may affect performance on less powerful devices or slower connections. Overall the site balances visual richness with usability.

Technical Performance

Technically, the site is ambitious, combining WebGL, CGI, and large media assets while maintaining smooth navigation. Such features can challenge loading times and metrics, however the design appears well-optimized for performance. Responsiveness seems strong, adapting across screen sizes, and though accessibility might benefit from additional refinements, the technical aspects make it possible for a great immersive experience.

Excellence Criteria

Chipsa design demonstrates excellence in multiple areas. Its design is outstanding, visually striking, cohesive, and memorable. Usability is generally strong, with minor potential friction on slower devices. Creativity is very high, combining storytelling, 3D, and interactive elements in innovative ways. The content is substantive, providing case studies, descriptions, and recognition that showcase the studio’s expertise and reinforce the narrative beyond purely visual appeal.

Conclusion

Overall, Chipsa design is an exemplar of immersive, narrative-driven web design. It illustrates how emotion, interactivity, and clarity can coexist to create a memorable user experience. Key takeaways include the importance of structured navigation, smooth microinteractions, and a cohesive visual identity, while maintaining performance and accessibility. The site serves as a benchmark for how high-quality design, storytelling, and technical expertise can work together to elevate a digital portfolio.
